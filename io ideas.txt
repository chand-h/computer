__controller:
5 button layout
4 buttons attached to 8-bit shift regs

layouts:
    for navigating:
	game:
		< \ > \ OPT \ A \ B
	TV remote style:
		counterclockwise 90d, top to bottom
		OPT \ UP \ DOWN \ SEL \ BACK
		
	flip-phone style:
		auto-detect
		A-F \ G-K \ L-P \ Q- \ V-Z
		or
		manual entry
		A-F \ G-L \ M-S \ T-Z \ E
    for typing and operation:
	coded style:
		fill out each slot then enter
		0-3 \ 0-3 \ 0-3 \ 0-3 \ E -> 256 codes
	chorded style:
		press and hold combo of first 4 then release
		do again then press enter
		takes last 2 chords
		0-1 \ 0-1 \ 0-1 \ 0-1 \ E -> 225 codes
